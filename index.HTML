Better Alternatives, No Compromise.

At NutriSip, we believe that "healthy" shouldn't be a chore and "traditional" shouldn't be boring. We started with Sattu, but our mission extends to every corner of the Indian pantry.

The Problem

Modern snacks are filled with maltodextrin, refined sugar, and artificial flavors that harm long-term health.

Our Solution

We use whole ingredients like Roasted Gram, Jaggery, and real Cocoa to create nutrient-dense foods kids love.

Whether we're working with Sattu, Millets, or Seeds, our goal remains the same: Transparent labels and Trusted nutrition.

The NutriSip Standard

Our four pillars of trust for every product we launch.

Zero Junk

No preservatives, artificial colors, or "nature identical" flavors. If we can't find it in a home kitchen, it's not in our pack.

No Refined Sugar

We exclusively use organic Jaggery or fruit-based sweeteners. No spikes, just sustained energy.

Kid Approved

Every flavor is tested by kids to ensure they love the taste as much as parents love the nutrition.

Lab Verified

Rigorous batch testing to ensure the protein and fiber content matches exactly what's on our label.
